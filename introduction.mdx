The Flipper Zero is a portable multi-tool device designed for interaction with wireless protocols, access control systems, hardware interfaces, and more. It supports Sub-GHz radio, Infrared, NFC, RFID, iButton, BadUSB scripting, and GPIO expansion — making it a versatile tool for security research, hardware tinkering, and automation.

This repository is UberGuidoZ's playground: a curated, community-driven collection of files, modifications, research, and tools for getting the most out of your Flipper Zero. Everything here has been made, modified, researched, or sourced by the community.

## Community resources

The Flipper Zero community is active and well-documented. The following resources are useful alongside this hub:

- **[Official Discord](https://discord.com/invite/flipper)** — the primary community chat, run by Flipper Devices.
- **[Official Docs](https://docs.flipperzero.one/)** — device documentation maintained by Flipper Devices.
- **[Awesome Flipper](https://github.com/djsime1/awesome-flipperzero)** — a curated list of apps, resources, and community projects.
- **[Official Forum](https://forum.flipperzero.one/)** — structured discussions, project showcases, and support threads.
- **[Flipper Zero Tutorials](https://github.com/jamisonderek/flipper-zero-tutorials)** — in-depth tutorials by codeallnight covering a wide range of Flipper topics.

<Note>
  Looking for Infrared (IR) codes? The full IR database lives at the [Flipper-IRDB repository](https://github.com/UberGuidoZ/Flipper-IRDB), which is maintained separately from this hub. Want to download just part of any repo? Use [DownGit](https://uberguidoz.github.io/DownGit/).
</Note>
