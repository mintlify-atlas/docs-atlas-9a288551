Whether you're replaying Sub-GHz signals, scripting BadUSB payloads, wiring up GPIO modules, or loading custom applications, you'll find tested files and guides here.

## Quick start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your Flipper Zero">
    Install qFlipper on your computer, connect your device via USB-C, and update to the latest firmware. See the [setup guide](/getting-started/setup) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your firmware">
    The official firmware is a solid baseline. Unleashed and Momentum unlock additional frequencies and features. Compare options in the [firmware guide](/getting-started/firmware-options).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load files from this repo">
    Download files by right-clicking the **Raw** button on GitHub and saving. Transfer them to your SD card using qFlipper or the Flipper mobile app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore by category">
    Browse [Sub-GHz signals](/signals/sub-ghz), [IR remotes](/signals/infrared), [BadUSB scripts](/badusb/overview), or [GPIO modules](/hardware/gpio-pinouts) to find what you need.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Looking for Infrared (IR) codes? The full IRDB lives at the [Flipper-IRDB repository](https://github.com/UberGuidoZ/Flipper-IRDB). Want to download just part of this repo? Use [DownGit](https://uberguidoz.github.io/DownGit/).
</Note>
